The Office is an American mockumentary sitcom television series that depicts the everyday work lives of office employees in the Scranton, Pennsylvania branch of the fictional Dunder Mifflin Paper Company.

Episodes

1. Pilot

The premiere episode introduces the boss and staff of the Dunder-Mifflin Paper Company in Scranton, Pennsylvania in a documentary about the workplace.

2. Diversity Day

Michael tells everyone it was his idea to hire a special consultant to teach racial tolerance and diversity.

3. Health Care

When Dwight is given the authority to choose a health plan for everyone, the power quickly goes to his head and infuriates his co-workers.

4. The Alliance

As downsizing rumours swirl, paranoia takes over the office. Dwight forms a `Survivor'-esque alliance with Jim against other employees.

5. Basketball

Michael is overly confident about his team's chances in a basketball game between the office staff and the warehouse workers.

6. Hot Girl

When an attractive purse saleswoman comes to the office, Michael and Dwight openly vie for her attention.

7. Gossip

It's time for the `Dundies', Dunder Mifflin's annual awards banquet, and the staff must endure the excruciating performance of their inept MC, boss Michael Scott.

8. Sexual Harassment

A drive to eradicate sexual harassment in the workplace coincides with the arrival of Todd Packer.
